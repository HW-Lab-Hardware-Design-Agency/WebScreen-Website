Technical Specifications

The WebScreen features a custom-designed board tailored to deliver top-notch performance and flexibility, closely aligning with the capabilities of advanced AMOLED display modules. This board is engineered to produce stunning visual outputs with vibrant colors and sharp resolution, making it perfect for displaying rich multimedia content and interactive applications.

Designed with versatility in mind, our board meets the needs of developers seeking a robust display solution that combines excellent image quality with extensive connectivity options.

Feature Specification
Material High-quality ABS plastic for serial production units and PLA for prototype models.
Memory Capacity 128Mb of serial flash memory for optimal performance.
Display Technology High-resolution AMOLED display, Resolution 240 X RGB X 536(H), capable of displaying 16.7 million colors.
Dimensions and Weight Compact design tailored for ease of use with variable weight depending on model specifications.
Durability Engineered to be splash, and dust resistant.
Processor Powered by the efficient ESP32S3 chip for seamless operation.
Connectivity Options Supports both Bluetooth and WiFi for flexible connectivity solutions.
Power Interface Utilizes a USB-C connector for power; designed without a battery to reduce weight and enhance portability.
Software Compatibility Compatible with Windows 10 or later, macOS, and popular Linux distributions. Requires WebScreen App v1.0 or newer.
Environmental Tolerance Operates effectively from 0° to 35° C. Robust to handle non-operating temperatures from -20° to 45° C.
Package Contents Each WebScreen is packaged with a USB-C cable for immediate setup and use.
