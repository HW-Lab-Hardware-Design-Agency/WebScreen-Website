Frequently Asked Questions

Explore common queries about WebScreen and discover how it can elevate your digital experience.

What makes WebScreen unique?

WebScreen is a unique secondary display that focuses on delivering crucial updates and personalized content directly to your desktop, enhancing both productivity and gaming experiences without interruptions.

How does WebScreen handle notifications differently than my phone or desktop?

WebScreen is designed to filter and display only the most critical notifications, ensuring that you receive important updates like urgent messages or reminders without the usual clutter found on phone or desktop notifications.

Can I customize what appears on my WebScreen?

Absolutely! WebScreen allows you to tailor the content displayed, from gaming alerts to business metrics, ensuring that the information that matters most to you is always front and center.

Does WebScreen require an internet connection for all its features?

Yes, while WebScreen can display static content like photos or schedules without internet, dynamic features such as real-time updates and social media integration require connectivity. For these features to function, an active internet connection is necessary.

What are the technical specifications of WebScreen?

WebScreen features a high-resolution AMOLED display, efficient ESP32S3 processor, and supports both Bluetooth and WiFi for flexible connectivity, packaged in a compact design with USB-C power.

How can WebScreen help me manage my day better than other devices?

WebScreen's design prioritizes your focus by filtering and displaying only the most essential notifications and reminders directly in your line of sight, thus minimizing distractions and enhancing your productivity and gaming experience.

Can I develop my own applications for WebScreen?

Yes, developers are encouraged to enhance WebScreen's functionality by creating and integrating custom applications, making it a highly versatile platform for all kinds of uses.
